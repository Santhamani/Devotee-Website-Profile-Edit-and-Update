Astrology


Indian Astrology is also known as Indian Vedic Astrology or Vedic Astrology. Vedic Astrology can be defined as the science explains in details the planetary movements and positions in respect to day and time, and their effects on 12 zodiac signs that influence the personality traits of humans. In short, it depends on the correct positions of the zodiacal fixed sun signs in regards to the place/location on the earth at given point of time.The term Jyotish means the science of light. It is very apt as Vedic astrology deals in astral light patterns that reflect our destiny and future.

Free Astrology Reading By Zodiac Signs

Aries is the first or head of signs of the Zodiac. It starts at Vernal Equinox and extends up to 30 degrees. Fiery by nature, cardinal and positive, Aries is a sign of short ascension. Sign Aries is also termed as bestial and sterile. It is an oriental sign denoting the beginning of the right ascension.

Taurus is the second sign of the Zodiac. It starts from 30 degrees of Vernal Equinox and extends up to 60 degrees. Earthy by nature, fixed and feminine, Taurus is a sign of short ascension. Sign Taurus is also termed as bestial and semi fruitful. Steady, practical and creative, Sign Taurus is known as the builder of Zodiac.
